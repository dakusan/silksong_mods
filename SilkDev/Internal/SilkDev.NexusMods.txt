[center][size=5]⚙️ [b]Installation[/b] ⚙️[/size]
[img=top]https://www.castledragmire.com/silksong/Line425.png[/img][/center][list=1]
[*]Install [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/hollowknightsilksong/mods/26]BepInEx[/url]
[*]Extract this mod’s zip into [b]Hollow Knight Silksong/BepInEx/plugins/dakusan/[/b]
[*]You should now have (for example): [b]Hollow Knight Silksong/BepInEx/plugins/dakusan/SilkDev.dll[/b]
[*]If you use my other mods — [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/hollowknightsilksong/mods/478]NoClip[/url] — move them into the same [b]dakusan[/b] folder.
[*]Run the game – done!
[/list]
You may also want to check out my [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/hollowknightsilksong/mods/478]NoClip mod[/url] for development help.

[center][size=5]🧩️ [b]Extracting Sprites[/b] 🧩️[/size]
[img=top]https://www.castledragmire.com/silksong/Line425.png[/img][/center][list]
[*]Set a shortcut in config (default: none – I ship it disabled because it’s included in many of my mods)
[*][b]L[/b]/[b]R[/b]/[b]M[/b]-click (when window open) [i]OR[/i] press shortcut key → Shows list ordered by cursor distance (closest auto-selected and on top)
[*]Config [b]Show boxes around sprites …[/b]: red outlines on hovered sprites, blue fill + name on closest [size=1][required for in-game-sprite clicking][/size]
[*]Click list item [i]OR[/i] in-game-sprite to display:[list]
[*]Left → single sprite from sprite sheet [size=1](may include surrounding sheet parts)[/size]
[*]Right → rendered sprite [size=1](can be twitchy)[/size]
[*]Middle → full sprite sheet [size=1](lots of sprites)[/size]
[*]Double-left → open sprite in Unity Explorer [size=1](if installed; list item only)[/size]
[*]Repeated clicks on animated sprites cycle through different frames
[/list]
[*]Left click image to save to: [i][b][color=green]PLUGIN_PATH[/color][/b]/Textures/[b][color=green]DATE[/color][/b]_[b][color=green]TIME[/color][/b]·[b][color=green]NAME[/color][/b].png[/i]
[*]Right click sheet to toggle highlight
[/list]
